I am a computer scientist and interdisciplinary researcher. My work focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence (machine learning and deep learning) with scientific discovery — particularly in solar physics and neuroscience.

I aim to develop reliable methods for forecasting extreme solar events and analyzing large-scale neuroimaging data using interpretable models. These problems are not only scientifically significant but have tangible consequences for national preparedness, public health, and technological infrastructure.
